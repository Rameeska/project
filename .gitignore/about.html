About Lassi Shop:


Lassi Shop –The story of a Quick Service Café is a small format 200 sqft space F&B Retail chain which exclusively focuses on the subcontinents popular traditional yoghurt based drink Lassi. We also serve fresh juices, mocktails and smoothies which are crafted exquisitely with the finest ingredients which will make you enjoy the fantastic array of flavours.

The company is the only multi-location, managed food retail venture which exclusively focuses on Indias core beverage Lassi and Mocktails & Which is into Franchising.

Lassi Shop is a new age concept Café that seeks to redefine successful food retailing worldwide by offering consumers Fresh, Healthy, and Tasty Authentically made Lassi and Mocktails at affordable price”.

Lassi Shop seeks to re-create the traditional Indian culture of celebrating lifes special moments with friends, Colleagues & Family through a nice cup of LASSI. We do so by serving our signature traditional Flavors of Lassi, A Lassi and Mocktails that is Thick and has the great taste.
